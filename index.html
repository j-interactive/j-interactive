Joe Griffiths

I'm a senior web developer and e-commerce architect based in Cheltenham, UK. I work part-time at SOZO, building and maintaining the backends and integrations behind high-volume retail sites: payments, search, email, and links into warehouse and stock systems. Right now I'm focused on Shopify, AI, and bespoke APIs.

I've been building for the web since 1999 and at SOZO since 2007, across bespoke PHP platforms, the infrastructure that runs them, and the e-commerce on top. Over the last year I've moved into Shopify extensibility: embedded apps and custom Functions handling checkout and discount logic.

My stack is PHP, JavaScript, SQL, Liquid, AWS, and Linux. I work extensively with Shopify Functions, Klaviyo, and Clerk.io, and I'm increasingly building AI into e-commerce workflows.

Before SOZO I freelanced as j-interactive (2003–2007), and started out at County Business Net as a senior developer and network administrator (1999–2003). BSc in Multimedia and Computing from the University of Gloucestershire.
